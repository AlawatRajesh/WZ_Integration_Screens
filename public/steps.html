Here are the key steps for using the Vue.js application:

  1. Open the Application: Access the webpage where the Vue app is running.
  2. Select a Workshop: Choose a workshop from the list to view the customers associated with it.
  3. Search Customers: Use the search bar to find customers by name or status.
  4. Apply Date Filters: Filter customers by Today, Yesterday, This Week, or This Month.
  5. View Customer Details: Click on a customer to see detailed info, including their receipt history (successful and failed payments).
  6. Navigate Pages: Use pagination controls to move between pages of customers.
  7. Close Modal: Close customer details by clicking the "Close" button or clicking outside the modal.
  8. Reset Filters: Clear any applied filters (search or date filters) to view the full customer list.
  9. Monitor Updates: Regularly check for any data updates or changes in customer status.
